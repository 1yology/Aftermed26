Conference Introduction

About AfterMed26

This conference serves as a scientific and interactive platform aimed at empowering healthcare students to explore their career paths more deeply and enhance their understanding of labor market requirements and various medical specialties.

The conference focuses on providing a comprehensive overview of key medical fields such as surgery, internal medicine, pediatrics, obstetrics and gynecology, and others through hosting a distinguished group of experienced physicians and consultants who will share their real-life experiences and professional advice.

The conference also aims to highlight different career development pathways, including clinical training, residency programs, scholarship opportunities, and working in both the public and private sectors. In addition, it explores emerging fields such as medicine and artificial intelligence, as well as entrepreneurship in healthcare.

The role of the conference extends beyond knowledge delivery to include the development of practical skills through workshops such as CV writing and interview preparation, helping participants build a strong professional profile. The conference primarily targets healthcare students, interns, and early-career physicians.

Why Should You Attend?

  • Gain deep insights into your future specialty through experienced physicians and consultants.
  • Define your career path with clarity and confidence.
  • Develop your CV and enhance your skills through interactive workshops.
  • Strengthen your chances of residency acceptance and job-market readiness.

Not Sure Which Specialty to Choose?

  • Discover specialties from real-world perspectives.
  • Get equipped with CV and interview skills.
  • Understand the steps for residency acceptance.

Conference Objectives

What the Conference Aims to Deliver

Exploring various medical specialties and understanding the scope and requirements of each path to make informed career decisions.

Enhancing early professional readiness by learning about residency programs, application processes, and admission requirements.

Developing application and employment skills, including building a professional medical CV and improving professional profiles.

Training on interview skills to increase confidence and effectiveness during applications.

Developing communication and networking skills to support academic and professional growth.

Providing opportunities for direct interaction with physicians, consultants, and peers to exchange experiences and guidance.

Expanding professional opportunities by exploring careers in both the public and private sectors, as well as scholarship opportunities.

Keeping up with modern advancements in medicine such as artificial intelligence and healthcare entrepreneurship.

Participating in interactive workshops designed to enhance practical and applied skills.

Workshop Description

CV Development & Medical Interview Skills Workshop

This comprehensive workshop equips participants with the skills needed to build a professional CV and master interview techniques with confidence and competence.

CV Writing

  • Learning the proper structure and organization of a medical CV.
  • Highlighting academic achievements and practical experiences effectively.
  • Avoiding common mistakes that reduce acceptance chances.
  • Hands-on improvement with direct feedback.
  • Tips on tailoring CVs for different opportunities.

Interview Skills

  • Understanding common questions in medical and residency interviews.
  • Effective answering strategies and professional self-presentation.
  • Managing stress and building confidence during interviews.
  • Mock interviews with immediate and constructive feedback.

Workshop Outcome

By the end of the workshop, each participant will have an improved, ready-to-use CV and a clear understanding of how to adapt it for different opportunities, with stronger confidence in interviews.
